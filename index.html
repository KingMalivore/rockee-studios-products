About Our Services

Rockee Studios specializes in providing premium digital content for video games, specifically focusing on virtual items and in-game currency for Epic Games titles. Our service enables gamers to enhance their gaming experience through our user-friendly platform.

We deliver all digital content instantly through our automated system, ensuring a seamless experience for our customers.

Our Products

How It Works

  1. Customers select the amount of credits they wish to purchase
  2. Payment is processed securely through Stripe (for credit/debit cards and digital wallets)
  3. Credits are instantly delivered to the customer's account
  4. Customers can use these credits to redeem in-game items and enhance their gaming experience

Payment Methods

For credit/debit card payments and digital wallets, we use Stripe as our payment processor:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

A small processing fee (3.5%) may apply to these transactions to cover payment processing costs.
